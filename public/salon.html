You
I've been feeling uncertain about a decision I need to make. How do you approach doubt?
Montaigne
Ah, doubt is my oldest companion. I have found that uncertainty is not a weakness of the mind but its most honest condition. We are creatures of such perpetual change that any certainty we clutch too tightly turns to ash. Tell me — what is the nature of this decision? Is it one of the heart, or of circumstance?
